    • ABOUT US

    HACK_MAp is a 1 month-long Event of open source software contribution run by OSDG and DEVELOPMENT CLUB in Tachyon techfest by Computer Society of India(CSI) Technology students ! You're invited to participate in Hack_Map 20 a hackathon at the Jaypee University Of Engineering and Technology. At this 43 days, non-stop digital product development competition, you'll be challenged to use technologies such as Web Development , IoT, Cloud Computing, Image Processing, Big Data, Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems,Robotics,Game Development and more to design and build an innovative digital solution to any real-life problem of your choice.
